Disinfo Fighters

A game about media and science literacy, and critical thinking education to combat misinformation

Ever tried to debate someone spreading disinformation? In the game, Disinfo Fighters, you'll play a historical case from Samoa in 2019, when measles vaccine misinformation there was rampant. You'll debate some of the worst spreaders of vaccine disinformation in the hopes of stopping a measles outbreak - while being supported by experts in logic and vaccine science. Do you have what it takes?

Disinfo Fighters is a game anyone can play for free. It was a game created to respond to the challenges students are facing with online misinformation and conspiracy theories. Help support the completion of the game. The trailer for the game and the game will be launching soon...
